About Us

Batra Engineering Co is amongst the leading manufacturers of Industrial & Spiral Wound Gaskets in India. The Company set up in 1987 has grown steadily through the past decades on the basis of its reputation on quality, accuracy and services. We strive to grow and expand with our motto of quick turnarounds and reliable service for our clients. We are equipped with modern machining technology.
At Batra Engineering Co, it is not just about what we make; it is also about what we can make. We specialize in a broad range of Gaskets and Industrial Gland packing. With semi-automatic machines, hydraulic press, lathe machines, spiral wound winding machines, we have the flexibility to complete nearly any job given to us.
Batra Engineering Co delivers innovative manufacturing solutions that meet the performance and quality demands of our regional, national, and global customers.We develop dedicated partnerships with our customers that result in mutual outcomes. We want to continue being known as one of the top companies that gives more emphasis on quality over anything else.

Our Vision


At Batra Engineering Co. our vision is to create the products to match the needs of our customers and provide timely solutions without compromising on productivity and quality. Our business idea supports our vision by offering a wide range of industrial gaskets and gland packing with a competitive edge in market. Our focus will always remain on high-quality products and customer satisfaction.
